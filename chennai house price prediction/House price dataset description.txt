1. price: The price of the property (in lakhs or millions, depending on the context).
2. area: The size of the property in square feet.
3. status: Indicates the current status of the property, such as:
   	Ready to move: The property is completed and available for immediate possession.
	Under Construction: The property is still under construction and not yet available for possession.
4. Bhk: The number of bedrooms, halls, and kitchens in the property (commonly referred to as BHK).
5. bathroom: The number of bathrooms in the property.
